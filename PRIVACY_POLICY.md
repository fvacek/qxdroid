# Privacy Policy for QXDroid

Last updated: 2025-01-01

QXDroid does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

## Information Collection and Use
The app does not collect personally identifiable information.
All data processed by the app remains on the user's device.

## Permissions
Any permissions requested by the app are used solely for providing core functionality and are not used to collect personal data.

## Third-Party Services
QXDroid does not use third-party services that collect user data.

## Changes to This Policy
This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

## Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository.
